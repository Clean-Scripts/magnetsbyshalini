Our Story

Turning Moments into Magnets.

Welcome to my Creative Space! What started as a hobby of capturing fleeting memories has blossomed into MagnetsByShalini.

I believe that everyday items—like your fridge—should be a canvas for your life’s best moments. Whether it's a family portrait, a divine idol, or a custom calendar, our mission is to keep your memories stuck with you, forever.

Each magnet is handcrafted with a focus on durability, a premium high-gloss finish, and most importantly, an emotional connection.
